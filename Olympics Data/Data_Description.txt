There are total 5 Excel sheets available in this project.
Every sheet has its own definite character.
Athelets - > All data related to Athelets is stored in this sheet.
Coaches - > Data related to Coaches available here, with country wise and sport wise.
Entries - > Gender wise data by countries.
Medals - > This is a dataset which has data realted to all the medals won, gold, silver, bronze and total.
Teams - > country wise teams data.
